The Curriculum

9 skills every solo founder must build

Distilled from what actually separates founders who survive from those who don't. Each one applies directly to building Vunagi&co as a solo founder scaling to a team.

01
Foundation
Sales & Persuasion

Revenue is oxygen. Nothing else matters first. Learn to articulate value clearly, handle objections without crumbling, and close without desperation.

Japan: Relationship-first. Trust before transaction. The long game.

02
Survival
Financial Literacy

More businesses die from cash flow problems than bad ideas. Know your margins, burn rate and break-even. Know where every yen goes before it goes there.

¥30M funding target. Work backwards from the number. Always.

03
Direction
Strategic Planning

A goal without a plan is a wish. Set the destination, map the milestones, build in review points. Know when to pivot versus when to persist.

Japan: Long-term thinking signals seriousness to investors.

04
Visibility
Marketing & Brand

Attention is the first currency. Build an audience before you need one. Tell a story that converts. Create a brand people want to belong to.

Vunagi&co is already proof. You understand this. Now formalise it.

05
Core Strength
Resilience & Self-Management

You are the business. Your standards are its standards. Your avoidance is its bottleneck. Your resilience is its foundation. This is the whole game.

Already your strongest quality. Protect it. Build on it daily.

06
Problem First
Customer Understanding

The product is never the starting point. The customer's problem is. Fall in love with the problem, not the solution. Talk to real people. Understand what keeps them up at night.

Ages 16-30, anime-native, growth-hungry. You are building for someone you understand.

07
The Only Thing
Execution & Shipping

Ideas are worthless. Execution is everything. Ship before you're ready. Learn from real feedback. Iterate in public. The longer you wait to launch, the longer you have nothing.

Kaizen — continuous improvement after shipping. Release, refine, repeat.

08
The Multiplier
Networking & Relationships

Your network is your net worth — especially true at the start with no track record. Build relationships before you need them. Be genuinely useful to people. The compound interest on relationships is extraordinary.

人脈 (jinmyaku) — personal connections are everything in Japanese business.

09
Solo → Team
Team Building & Leadership

You are the vision. But you cannot be the operations, the finance, the design, the fulfilment and the foundation simultaneously. A solo founder who cannot attract people around the mission stays solo forever.

The team Vunagi&co needs: Operations · Marketing · Finance · Japan partner · Thailand partner.
